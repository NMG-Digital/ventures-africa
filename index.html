About us

For six decades, Nation Media Group has been the largest media company in East & Central Africa. Then it retooled as a modern content company. There are great opportunities in digital content, meaning anything that informs, educates or entertains

To capture the opportunities, N2 Ventures Africa was created as a self-standing venture arm of Nation Media. N2 builds, partners and invests in African start-ups, creating a transformative content ecosystem

Every day we reach millions of people and deploy incredible human talent to support new products. We are inventing the future of digital content in Africa
